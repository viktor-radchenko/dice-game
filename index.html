The main goal of this game is to reach 100 points by rolling dice. Players take turns rolling dice and sum the result. In case the number on dice is 1, player looses all unsaved points and passes the turn to another player. To save your points, click 'Hold' button, and all current points will be added to your score. Enjoy!
